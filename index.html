About Us

Curriculum

Our courses are for those who are interested in pursuing a carrier in automotive engineering design and product development. The goal of our every program is to convert students into domain specialist. After completion of a program, you will not only be able to design and develop most components of your domain but also become specialist with a comprehensive system-wide knowledge area like of which are extremely rare and highly regarded throughout the industry. Students will also learn in-depth about vehicle Engineering as whole, general design practices, automotive materials and how to select them, standard product development processes, APQP and PPAP.  


We aim to provide the strongest training tools in existence in the automotive industry to upgrade young professionals into product development specialists through our Micro master and PG programs.  It is said that nobody knows everything, but when it comes to engineering a vehicle, our students will be as close as you'll get. Hence, it won't be an exaggeration to say that our programmes are unique in a way that there’s nothing like it out there.

Our vision and core values

Our founders' vision is to change the current employment landscape & create opportunities for freshers and young professionals by enhancing their knowledge and upgrading them into being a specialist in their respective trade. Our core value is that education should be of the highest of quality, affordable & accessible.

A commitment of uncompromising quality:
All of our educators are industry experts and wel renowned in the automotive engineering field. Our faculty bring their wealth of experience into their lessons. To ensure highest of quality, we made it our policy that every course will be taught by at least two lecturers. 

Levels and learning goals

Every  programme is structured as a four-level system and each level is designed to meet different learning goals,

 Introductory level:

Student has a basic idea of vehicle architecture and more confidence to choose his or her specialization.
Intermediate Level:

Student can now design general components (brackets, hose, panels etc.), select materials, draw and able to finalize his or her specialization.
Professional Level:

Student is now able to design most components within the subsystem they opted for and carry out general design engineering activities.

Specialist Level: 

Student has extensive in-depth knowledge of his or her function group, relevant child domains and comprehensive system level knowledge, that goes beyond his day to day work.
